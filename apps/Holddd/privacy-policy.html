Privacy Policy for Holddd

Last Updated: April 2026

TiTil Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Holddd — the mindful spending app (the "App"). Please read this carefully. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data

  • Email address — used only for sign-in via Email OTP or Google Sign-In. We do not store your password.

Purchase Records

  • Items you add to Holddd (name, price, timer duration, status: pending / saved / bought)
  • Your decisions and timestamps — stored securely in your personal account

Personalization Data

  • Survey answers: spending goal, monthly range, spending category
  • Optional hourly wage (used locally to calculate Time-to-Earn)

App Preferences

  • Theme (light/dark), language, currency — stored locally on your device via SharedPreferences

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Bank account or credit card information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any data for advertising purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the App (saving your items, decisions, and statistics)
  • To personalize your experience based on your survey answers
  • To validate and manage your Pro subscription via RevenueCat
  • To send local notifications (purchase deadline reminders) — entirely on-device, no data leaves your phone
  • To generate your weekly digest and spending statistics — computed from your own data only

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account and purchase data are stored securely on Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled — meaning only you can access your own data. No other user or admin can read your records.

App preferences (theme, language, currency, wage) are stored only on your device and never leave it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the service providers listed in Section 2 strictly to operate the App's features.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by:

  • Emailing us at contact@titilstudio.com

We will delete all your data within 30 days of your request.

7. Children's Privacy

Holddd is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@titilstudio.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
