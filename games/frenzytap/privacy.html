Privacy Policy for FrenzyTap

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

TiTil Studio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our mobile game, FrenzyTap (the "Game"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

When you play FrenzyTap, we may collect information about you in a few different ways:

  • Google Play Games Services Data: To provide features such as Achievements and Leaderboards, FrenzyTap integrates with Google Play Games Services. When you sign in with your Google account, we access your Google Play Games profile (such as your username, avatar, and scores). We do not store your Google password. This data is handled directly by Google.
  • Gameplay Data: We collect non-personal, anonymous data regarding your gameplay (e.g., high scores, levels reached, and time played) to display on Leaderboards and unlock Achievements.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers) solely for the purpose of serving ads and improving game performance.

2. Third-Party Services

FrenzyTap uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services have their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for authenticating users, Achievements, and Leaderboards. (Google Privacy Policy)
  • AdMob (Google Ads): Our game may display advertisements provided by AdMob. AdMob may use cookies or mobile advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's settings. (Google Ads Privacy Policy)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Game (e.g., saving your high scores).
  • Enable integration with Google Play Games Services for Leaderboards and Achievements.
  • Serve advertisements to support the development of the Game.
  • Monitor and analyze game performance to fix bugs and improve user experience.

4. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We only share information with the third-party service providers mentioned above (Google Play and AdMob) strictly for the operational functionality of the Game and advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention and Security

We do not store your personal data on our own servers. All gameplay progress, authentication, and advertising data are securely processed and stored by Google's robust infrastructure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

FrenzyTap is a general audience game and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
